# 🧠 Ayurveda SaaS Feature vs Pricing Gap Map

## Step 1 — First Principle Reality

Almost all Ayurveda software today sells:

> **Digitisation of clinic operations**

NOT:

> **Growth, outcomes, or intelligence**

That mismatch creates pricing inefficiency — and opportunity.

# 🥇 What Current Tools Actually Sell

Let's map real examples using verified positioning.

---

## AyurGrid

* Clinic management + digital records + scheduling
* Ayurveda-specific workflows
* Entry pricing starts around **₹7,000/year** ([Techjockey][1])

**Value Delivered:** operational efficiency.

**Gap:** no strong revenue or retention engine.

---

## Clinicia (Ayurveda)

* Dosha-based EMR
* Patient engagement messaging
* Multi-user cloud platform ([Clinicia][2])

**Value Delivered:** clean workflow + documentation.

**Gap:** engagement ≠ growth intelligence.

---

## AyushEHR

* Activity or visit-based pricing (₹10 per visit model) ([Ayurveda Cloud][3])
* Focus on record standardisation and EHR infrastructure.

**Value Delivered:** structured data.

**Gap:** no monetisation layer for clinics.

---

## PappyJoe

* Full clinic/hospital operations stack ([Pappyjoe Software][4])

**Value Delivered:** administration scale.

**Gap:** feels like ERP, not practice growth system.

---

# 🔥 The Core Insight (Why Pricing Stays Low)

Indian Ayurveda clinics pay:

* ₹1k–₹3k/month willingly
* Resist ₹5k+/month strongly

Why?

Because software currently saves **time**, not **makes money**.

Software that:

* saves time → cost tool → low pricing ceiling
* increases revenue → growth tool → high pricing ceiling

---

# 🧩 The Missing Value Layers (Nobody Fully Owns Yet)

## 1️⃣ Patient Journey Intelligence (Almost Zero Competition)

Ayurveda treatments are long-cycle.

Typical journey:

```
Consult → Therapy → Lifestyle → Follow-ups → Maintenance
```

No SaaS deeply tracks:

* prakriti evolution,
* therapy effectiveness,
* compliance tracking,
* relapse prediction.

👉 Massive differentiation layer.

---

## 2️⃣ Revenue Engine Layer (Biggest Gap)

Clinics struggle with:

* missed follow-ups,
* therapy drop-offs,
* unused packages,
* low lifetime value.

No major Ayurveda SaaS provides:

* automated reactivation,
* therapy upsell logic,
* health subscription billing,
* WhatsApp conversion funnels.

Yet this directly impacts revenue.

---

## 3️⃣ Ayurveda Intelligence Layer (Category Creator)

Current software records:

> "Patient is Vata dominant."

Future software should answer:

> "Based on 1,200 similar cases, this protocol improves outcomes."

Nobody owns this yet.

---

# 💰 Pricing Inefficiency (Where Opportunity Exists)

Here's the hidden pricing paradox:

| Feature Type         | Clinics Expect Price      |
| -------------------- | ------------------------- |
| EMR                  | ₹1k/month                 |
| Billing              | Included                  |
| Reports              | Included                  |
| Patient acquisition  | ₹5k–₹20k/month acceptable |
| Retention automation | ₹3k–₹10k/month acceptable |

Meaning:

👉 Clinics will pay **5–10× more** for growth than for management.

# 🧭 The Real Competitive Positioning Insight

You should **not build "another Ayurveda EMR."**

The winning positioning is:

> **Clinic Growth Platform for Ayurveda Practices**

Because:

* EMR = replacement market
* Growth = expansion market

Expansion markets price higher and churn less.
